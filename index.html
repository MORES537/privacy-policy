Privacy Policy for MindMend

Last updated: July 5, 2025

MindMend does not collect or track personal audio data. All audio inputs remain on the user's device and are not stored or transmitted to any third-party services.

User account data is stored locally using device identifiers (AsyncStorage) and is not connected to third-party services. We do not share or sell user data.

Contact us at mindmend.app.help@gmail.com for any questions or concerns.
