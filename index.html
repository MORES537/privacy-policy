MindMend is a privacy-focused mental wellness app. This policy explains what data is processed and why.

Summary

  • Your journal, history, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • When you request an AI response, your input is sent to OpenAI’s API solely to provide that response. We do not run our own servers.
  • No analytics SDKs, cookies, or third-party advertising.

Data We Process

On Your Device (Local Only)

  • Thought-reframing history
  • Emotional journal entries and mood tags
  • Language and app preferences

You can clear local data anytime in the app or by uninstalling the app.

AI Processing with OpenAI

Text you submit (and, if enabled, voice/audio for transcription) is transmitted to OpenAI solely to generate the requested response. OpenAI does not use API data to train its models by default. Per OpenAI’s policy, API data may be retained for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring and then deleted, unless legally required to retain it. Check OpenAI’s data policy for updates.

Learn more: OpenAI — Your Data · OpenAI Privacy Policy

Permissions We Request (Purpose)

  • Microphone / Speech Recognition — Optional voice input for reframing and exercises.
  • Photo Library (read/add) — Only if you choose to attach or export images related to your journal. Nothing is uploaded by us.
  • Camera — Only if you decide to take a photo for your journal.

Children’s Privacy

MindMend is not directed to children under 13.

Your Choices

  • Clear your local history and journal entries at any time in the app.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.

Security

Local data remains on your device. Data sent to OpenAI is transmitted over HTTPS. We do not sell personal data or use it for advertising.
