MindMend – Privacy Policy

Your privacy is very important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Information We Collect

  • No personal data is collected or stored on our servers.
  • All user input remains on your device and is not transmitted externally.
  • Subscriptions are handled securely via Apple’s In-App Purchase system.

Usage of Data

Your data is only used locally for app functionality such as saving emotional entries or preferences. We do not sell, share, or track any of your information.

Terms of Use (EULA)

Subscription

MindMend PRO is offered as a yearly auto-renewable subscription via Apple. The subscription includes access to:

  • Unlimited AI responses with multiple styles
  • Guided exercises with audio and illustrations
  • Emotional journal and full response history

Payment & Renewal

Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. Your subscription will automatically renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

Canceling

You can manage and cancel your subscription by going to your App Store account settings after purchase.
